01 — About

Background & Approach

My route into cyber security isn't conventional — and that's a genuine strength. After 15 years directing my own commercial business, I understand how organisations actually operate: the human decisions behind risk, the gap between policy and practice, and what it takes to make compliance stick in the real world.

Before that, I spent three years as a Senior IT Recruitment Consultant at Venn Group, placing technology professionals specifically within NHS and public sector organisations. That experience gave me deep familiarity with regulated, compliance-driven environments that most junior candidates simply don't have.

I passed CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) in February 2026 and have since built a practical GRC portfolio — including an ISO 27001 Gap Analysis, a NIST CSF-aligned Incident Response Plan, and a full UK GDPR Data Audit & DPIA. These are available for download below.

"Security isn't just technical. It's behavioural, organisational, and human. That's where my background gives me an edge."
