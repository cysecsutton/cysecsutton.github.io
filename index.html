01 — About

Background &
Approach

My route into cyber security isn't conventional — and that's a strength. After years running my own business in the trades, I understand how organisations actually operate: the human decisions behind risk, the gap between policy and practice, and what it takes to implement change in the real world.

That practical foundation shapes how I approach security. I don't just understand frameworks — I understand why they fail and how to make them stick. Whether it's an ISO 27001 gap analysis or a risk register, I bring the same methodical, outcome-focused mindset I've applied throughout my career.

I passed CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) in February 2026 and am actively deepening my GRC specialism through ISO 27001 Lead Implementer study, with a focus on landing a role where compliance and risk management are core to the mission.
